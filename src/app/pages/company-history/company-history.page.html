Company History

Plantita

Plantita Company is the largest locally owned and operated provider of plant services in the Philippines. Our diverse
and highly experienced staff has aided us in achieving this position of leadership. Even after more than 5 years,
one thing remains constant: our desire to create the
most desirable environment for every technology facility, hotel, retail store, or your home. We foster an environment
in which you can work, play, and grow. Most species on Earth would perish if plants did not exist. Both the air we breathe
and the food we eat rely on the continued existence of plant life on our planet.
